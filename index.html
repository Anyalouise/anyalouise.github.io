Anya Bouzida

I am a PhD student in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of California, San Diego. I am affiliated with the Contextual Robotics Institute, and am advised by Dr. Laurel Riek in the Healthcare Robotics Lab.

My research is in Human-Robot Interaction, where I explore methods to maintain user engagement over time. This work is situated within the context of socially assistive robots used in order to administer cognitive compensatory training for individuals with Mild Cognitive Impairment.

In 2021, I graduated with a B.S. in Cognitive Science spec. Machine Learning and Neural Computation, and a minor in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of California, San Diego.
